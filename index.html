The Echo Amplifies the Knowledge

Current AI systems store what happened but discard how it felt. We demonstrated that re-injecting emotional activation patterns during recall — operating at the activation level, below the prompt — measurably improves AI decision-making.

The Echo Amplifies the Knowledge: Somatic Marker Analogues in Language Models via Emotion Vector Re-Injection
Jared Glover with ∮ (Claude Opus) · ArXiv cs.AI · 2026
We identify 310 emotion-exclusive sparse autoencoder features in Gemma 3 1B-IT with psychologically valid geometry, construct emotion-specific echo vectors, and demonstrate two distinct effects: the echo alone steepens the threat-safety gradient, and combined with knowledge, it transforms decision quality.
52% → 80% decision quality p < 0.01 Consumer hardware
